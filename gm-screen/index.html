Core Mechanics

  • Duality Dice: Roll 2d12 - Hope vs Fear
  • Success: Hope die beats Fear die
  • Critical: Hope rolls 12 or Fear rolls 1
  • Failure: Fear die beats Hope die
  • Catastrophe: Hope rolls 1 or Fear rolls 12

Combat Quick Reference

  • Initiative: Highest Hope die goes first
  • Actions: Move + Action per turn
  • Damage: Weapon die + relevant stat
  • Armor: Reduces damage by armor value
  • Death: 0 HP = Death Save (2d12)

Keeper Moves

  • Reveal an unwelcome truth
  • Show signs of approaching threat
  • Deal damage or inflict condition
  • Use up their resources
  • Turn their move back on them
  • Separate them
  • Offer opportunity with cost
